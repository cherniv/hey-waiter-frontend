Waiter.Live Future of Service(?) What is Waiter.Live? In fact, the same as well known "restaurant table button" solution, but it does not require you to install any buttons, nor your waiters need to wear pagers that you buy for money. "Waiter.Live" takes advantage on ubiquity of smartphones and does the same, but for free! Comparison No equipment 1 .3 Just an old good place where you wave your hand in the hope that waiter will notice you Buzzer button 2 .6 Expensive: for example a pack of 20 buttons, display and 10 pagers(each waiter needs to have one), the entire solution costs over $700-$1500 USD Unreliable: button's batteries may drain or it might break in the middle of the day, rendering your client furious Some drunk prankers may just steal the button, for no reason Waiter.Live 3 1 Totally free Easy to deploy Gathers valuable statistics of each timme anyone orders anything, so you can analyze the workflow of your restaurant and optimize accordingly Every time guests scan the QR code - not only it calls for a waiter, but also promotes a link of the restaurant's Instagram page(or any other link) into the phone's call page, increasing chances of them to follow/subscribe/visit the page Getting started Waiter.Live is very simple platform. To get started, do the following: Log in to manager Add your tables Design and print QR codes, install them on your tables Make all your waiters install "Waiter.Live" app (don't worry - guests do NOT have to install any app!) Everything is free! As for guests, it's totally efortless for them: all they need to do is just to scan the code using their smartphone cameras and immediately it sends a signal to waiter's app. They do not need to install any app, which is important today (people don't like to install superfluous apps) Something else Some other things that we maybe should add here?
